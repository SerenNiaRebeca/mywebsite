A little more about me

Tea addict, internet nut, snow lover and keen photographer.

I was born and raised in South Wales and have been lucky enough to have lived and worked in England, America, and France! (As you can see I like to explore this big wide world we live in).

I grew up being involved in music competitions, stage shows and dance camps, however I soon learnt my creative side was better utilised elsewhere - all thanks to my 2 left feet!

I've been able to experience a variety of roles, from volunteering to an industrial placement. The most interesting interview question I've been posed would have to be, 'Why would someone not want to work with you?'. Although I'd like to think my sincere, friendly, curious and ambitious personality traits will always triumph, I must confess to my sometimes perfectionist OCD tendencies or even my constant need for to-do lists!

I would love to live in a world in which we celebrate every tiny victory. Despite my optimism, I've come to realise that although there will always be good and bad going on in the world, life is about our day to day interactions. One of the best pieces of advice I've heard comes from a Mr Will Smith...

"If you're not making someone else's life better, then you are wasting your time".
